Gold Gold ore is a mineral block found in the Overworld and the Nether. It is a source of gold, which can be smelted from the ore. Gold ore is most commonly found in veins of 1–9 blocks, and generates between levels 0 and 32 in the Overworld, and between levels 10 and 117 in the Nether.
Copper Copper ore is a mineral block found in the Overworld. It is a source of copper, which can be smelted from the ore. Copper ore is most commonly found in veins of 1–16 blocks, and generates between levels 0 and 96.
Iron Iron ore is a mineral block found in the Overworld. It is a source of iron, which can be smelted from the ore. Iron ore is most commonly found in veins of 1–10 blocks, and generates between levels 0 and 63.
Coal Coal ore is a mineral block found in the Overworld. It is a source of coal, which can be smelted from the ore. Coal ore is most commonly found in veins of 1–17 blocks, and generates between levels 0 and 127.
Emerald Emerald ore is a mineral block found in the Overworld. It is a source of emeralds, which can be mined from the ore. Emerald ore is most commonly found in veins of 1 block, and generates between levels 4 and 31 in mountain biomes.
Amethyst Amethyst is a mineral block found in the Overworld. It is a source of amethyst shards, which can be mined from the ore. Amethyst generates in geodes, which are spherical structures that can be found underground.
Diamonds Diamond ore is a mineral block found in the Overworld. It is a source of diamonds, which can be mined from the ore. Diamond ore is most commonly found in veins of 1–10 blocks, and generates between levels 0 and 15.
