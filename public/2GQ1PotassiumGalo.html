Name Image Information Video references
Gold Ooooooooo shinyyyyyy. Gold ore is most commonly found in veins of 1–9 blocks, and generates between levels 0 and 32 in the Overworld, and between levels 10 and 117 in the Nether.
Copper Honestly not really that useful, but it is trying its best. Copper ore is most commonly found in veins of 1–16 blocks, and generates between levels 0 and 96.
Iron Possibly the most useful ore in the game. Tools and machinery. Iron ore is most commonly found in veins of 1–10 blocks, and generates between levels 0 and 63.
Coal Coal will most likely be your best source of fuel for the early game. Trust me, you'll always be looking for more. Coal ore is most commonly found in veins of 1–17 blocks, and generates between levels 0 and 127.
Emerald Emerald can be used to trade with villages for goods (cough cough Tomas). Emerald ore is most commonly found in veins of 1 block, and generates between levels 4 and 31 in mountain biomes.
Amethyst Same with copper if not more pointless. Cool texture though. Amethyst generates in geodes, which are spherical structures that can be found underground.
Diamonds The one and only. Need not any introduction. Diamond ore is most commonly found in veins of 1–10 blocks, and generates between levels 0 and 15.
